Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the best way to find the best coffee on campus?
Try the Cafes right outside campus, Mysk and Blacksmith have some of the best coffee!.
How do I survive group projects without losing my mind?
Communication is key! Plus, bringing snacks can help keep the mood light and everyone happy.
What’s a must-try food on campus?
You can't go wrong with Sushi Counter! They often have the best variety of food that keep everyone coming back for more.
What’s the best way to make friends in my classes?
Sit next to someone new and strike up a conversation! Everyone's in the same boat, and you'll be surprised at how quickly you bond.
Is it okay to take naps between classes?
Absolutely! Just find a comfy spot and recharge. Napping is an art form in college.
What should I do if I get lost on campus?
Don’t panic! Just ask someone for directions or use your phone’s map. Everyone’s been there, and it’s a great way to meet new people.
